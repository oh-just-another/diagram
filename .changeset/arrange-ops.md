Arrange operations for the selection. **Flip** mirrors the selection about its bounding-box centre — horizontal (`Shift+H`) and vertical (`Shift+V`); a single shape flips about its own centre. Available from the selection property panel and the right-click menu. New engine API: `Editor.flipSelection(axis)`.
